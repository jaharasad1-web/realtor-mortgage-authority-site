Common Questions

Do I need to speak with Jahar before looking at homes?

It is best to talk before touring homes so your budget, loan options, cash-to-close, and negotiation strategy are clear from the beginning.

Can Jahar help with both the home search and the mortgage?

Yes. Jahar combines Realtor representation and mortgage loan officer guidance so buyers can coordinate both sides of the transaction.

Is there a charge to read these guides?

No. These website guides are built as free education resources for consumers.
