Frequently Asked Questions

Is the masterclass really free?

Yes. There is no cost to register or attend.

Will I be pressured to buy a home?

No. The class is educational and designed to help you understand the process and your possible next steps.

Can I ask questions?

Yes. The live session includes dedicated time for questions and answers.

What if I am not ready to buy yet?

You are still welcome. Learning early can help you prepare your budget, credit, savings, and timeline.
