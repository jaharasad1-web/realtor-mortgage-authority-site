Buyer Strategy

A strong buyer plan starts before the showing. The right strategy includes budget review, mortgage structure, property type, inspection comfort level, seller concession strategy, and a clear understanding of monthly payment.

  • Get pre-approved before touring.
  • Know your payment comfort zone.
  • Review DPA, FHA, VA, USDA, and conventional options.
  • Understand due diligence, earnest money, inspections, and appraisal risk.
